Thank you for your membership contribution. You've shown that you understand the issues facing the mountain biking community, and that you're committed to making our sport better near your home and around the world.

Organizations like yours are the backbone of our cause and you're not alone. IMBA's global network is more than 80,000 strong with support from individuals, clubs, bicycle retailers and companies who share your commitment. We are bonded by a common passion for riding.

Ride assured, IMBA will leverage every cent of your membership dollar to create, enhance and preserve great trail opportunities for mountain biking.

We'll represent you in the halls of government, where key trail access decisions are made. We also have five Regional Directors working hard to implement many of IMBA's excellent programs near you.

From the famed Subaru/IMBA Trail Care Crews and Trail Solutions consulting to lobbying on Capitol Hill and IMBA Epics, our programs are effective advocacy tools thanks to the support of partners like you.

We have expanded our Team IMBA program and we encourage partners to put together a fundraising team for a race near you or at one of our Signature Events. Go to go.imba.com/teamimba for more information.
